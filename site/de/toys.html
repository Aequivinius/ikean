Hana Chiro Sato

Transport yourself to the elegant world of the Tale of Genji with our exquisite chashaku, 'Hana Chiro Sato.' Crafted with meticulous attention to detail, this traditional Japanese tea scoop embodies the grace and beauty of Genji's era.

In the delicate hands of tea connoisseurs, the chashaku serves as an essential tool for measuring the perfect amount of matcha powder, ensuring a harmonious and authentic tea experience. Each scoop is a testament to centuries of tea ceremony tradition, evoking a sense of reverence and connection to Japan's rich cultural heritage.

Whether you're a seasoned matcha enthusiast or a newcomer to the world of Japanese tea, 'Hana Chiro Sato' promises to elevate your tea ritual to new heights. Indulge in the artistry and refinement of the Tale of Genji as you savor every sip of your perfectly prepared matcha. Bring a piece of Japanese history into your home and let the timeless elegance of 'Hana Chiro Sato' enrich your tea journey.
