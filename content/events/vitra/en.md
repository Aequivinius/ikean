As the cherry blossoms reach full bloom, the Vitra Campus takes on an enchanting aura. This year, we invite you to join guided tours focusing on Japanese architecture, followed by a traditional Japanese tea ceremony in Tadao Ando's Conference Pavilion The tours take place on Tuesday, 31 March and Saturday, 4 April at 1.30 pm (German) and 3 pm (English).

Programme:

* One-hour guided tour of Japanese architecture on the Vitra Campus with a Japanese guide
* Followed by a traditional Japanese tea ceremony in the Ando Conference Pavilion

The price of € 59 per person includes:

* One-hour guided tour of Japanese architecture on the Vitra Campus with a Japanese guide
* One-hour introduction to the Japanese tea ceremony with demonstration on a traditional tatami platform
* One cup of matcha and one Japanese sweet per person
